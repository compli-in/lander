Compliance does not fail during audits.

It fails before them.

Quietly.

A missed access review.

A delayed onboarding step.

A log that was never checked.

Individually, nothing breaks.

Collectively, the system drifts.

There is no signal.

No alert.

No immediate consequence.

So it continues.

Until the audit.

Then everything surfaces at once:

- Gaps  
- Inconsistencies  
- Missing evidence  

The failure looks sudden.

It is not.

It accumulated.

Slowly.

Without interruption.

Without enforcement.

Without visibility tied to execution.

By the time it is visible, it is already systemic.

Compliance does not fail loudly.

It fails when nothing happens.
